Message to my Fans

Hello my dear loving friends. I hope you all stay healthy and stay safe. I want to share few words with you. You all know that singing is my life. It has always been my life. It will always be my life. Music is a therapy. It is a communication far more powerful than words, far more immediate, far more efficient. I believe musicians have a duty, a responsibility to reach out, to share your love or pain with others. Sometimes music is the only medicine the heart and soul need. Someone once asked me "Why do you love music so much ?". I replied "Because it's the only thing that stays when everything and everyone is gone". Music expresse that which can't be said and on which it is impossible to be silent. a painter paints picture on canvas But musicians paint their pictures on silence. Stay safe.

DHVANI BHANUSHALI
